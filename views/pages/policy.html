Invitation and announcement process:

When purchasing invitations and/or announcements from us, you will first add the sample that you like to your cart. Then, you will be taken to your shopping cart where you can either click the button to keep shopping or you can complete your order. When you are ready to complete your order, you will fill out the order form in the shopping cart and, at that point, you might notice that you are not paying the full cost of the invitations and/or announcements yet but just paying for our time and supplies to get your custom invitations and/or announcements started and samples made. After the order is received and you have paid your starting fee, you will be sent an email that will tell you what we need from you (for example: pictures, names, dates, addresses) in order to create your invitations and/or announcements. Then, after we receive your information, we will begin to make your custom sample. It will take us anywhere from a few hours to a few days depending on how busy we are and how many orders we have to fill. After your sample has been created, we will send an email to the email address that you provided when placing the order that will contain a link for you to log in to preview and approve the sample. Please, at this point, double check the text and colors and make sure that it all looks correct. We would hate to send you invitations and/or announcements that have names spelled incorrectly or the wrong dates or addresses. If everything looks correct, then you will approve the sample and pay the rest of the invitation and/or announcement cost. If something looks off to you or if the information is incorrect, check the “No, let’s make some changes” box and you can let us know what changes need to be made. Once the sample is approved and payment is received, we will send your invitations and/or announcements to the printer. Then, we will inspect the product when it comes back from the printer to assure quality before the product is shipped to you. After this, we will ship the product to you and send you a message that the product is on its way to you. And finally, you will receive your product! After receiving your product, if you would like, please leave us a comment, review, or email so we can know how we did. We would love to hear from you!

If you have any other questions about this process that we use for invitations and/or announcements, please contact us at contactus@tiedwithstrings.com and we will get back to you with answers as soon as we can.
